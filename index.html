We are We-Can

We imagine a world where anyone can recognize and intervene in an overdose situation, where anyone can save lives with accessible naloxone, and where no one believes the stigma of substance use disorder or an overdose.

We are creating a new channel for opioid education and community resources.

We are 100% funded by a grant from LifeSpan and this is a completely non-profit effort.
