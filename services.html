Clinical Services

We offer a wide range of clinical services in our pharmacy practice for your convenience

Compounding Appointments Genetic Testing Education Immunizations

Compounding

We offer affordable compounded medications for children, adults and animals. Our pharmacists work directly with your provider to streamline your experience and ensure you receive a precise, individualized product.

Appointments

Our specialists are here, in the community, to provide you with accesible, high quality health care. We offer services by walk-in or appointment, and can assist with many different health needs from disease management to insurance assistance.

Genetic testing

Many people wonder what information lies stored within their own DNA. Let us help you discover more about your individual health needs through our personalized genetic testing, consultation, and interpretation, all available in the same convenient location. Genetic consultation has never been more accessible!

Education

Many patients and parents frequently wonder what they can do to improve their personal health. Here at Children's Choice Pharmacy, we offer community education on different common diseases on a rotating schedule. We offer many opportunities to maximize your ability to manage you and your family's health.

Immunizations

From measles to the flu, staying up to date with vaccines is very important to protecting your child and the community from widespread diseases. We offer convenient immunizations at our pharmacy, without a prescription or appointment.
